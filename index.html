03 — How It Works

Transparent
by default.

Transparency isn't a perk — it's the foundation. Here's what joining Stack looks like.

01
You join the studio
Join the roster. You stay with the studio and get matched to projects that fit your skillset.
02
Stack wins the work
Clients hire Stack. When a brief lands, the studio assembles the right team from its roster.
03
You do what you do best
Focus on the craft. Stack handles coordination, comms, contracts, and delivery.
04
Portfolio rights
You keep full portfolio credit for everything you create. Stack grows its body of work. Everyone wins.
